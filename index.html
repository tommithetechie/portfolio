My Skills

What I can do

I specialise in building modern, responsive websites using HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript. Whether it's crafting pixel-perfect layouts or ensuring a seamless experience across all devices, I bring a strong eye for detail and a passion for quality code to every project.

I'm experienced with popular front-end frameworks and libraries, and I'm comfortable working alongside back-end developers to integrate APIs and dynamic content. I enjoy finding elegant solutions to complex UI challenges.

About Me

A little about me

Hi, I'm Tommi — a front-end web developer with a passion for building things on the web. I've been coding for several years and love the challenge of turning a blank canvas into something functional and beautiful. I'm constantly learning and keeping up with the latest trends in web technology.
